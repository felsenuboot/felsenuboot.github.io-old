History & Values of van de Velde

Van de Velde (further also “the company”) is a public company located in Schellebelle, Belgium. The company was established by Achiel and Margaretha van de Velde in 1919. It celebrated its 100th anniversary recently. Van de Velde started off by only selling corsets on the Belgian market. It did not take long before they decided to do their first international step to the Netherlands. Dutch and Flemish customer demographics are relatively similar making the expansion easier. Van de Velde launched its first brand named “Marie Jo” in 1981. With the launch of the new brand van de Velde had clearly marked its new strategy. Along the way they would acquire more and more brands. Van de Velde was able to acquire the German company PrimaDonna in 1990. Its third brand Marie Jo L’Aventure was launched in 1997. The year also marked another milestone for van de Velde. The company went public with its Initial Public Offering (IPO) on the Brussels Stock Exchange. It went from being a privately owned family company to being a public company.
